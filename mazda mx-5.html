MAZDA MX-5


    The Mazda MX-5 (also known as the Miata) is a lightweight, two-seat sports car known for its agility, fun-to-drive nature, and affordability. First introduced in 1989, it has become an iconic roadster with a simple, compact design and an emphasis on driver engagement.

    Performance:

  • Powered by a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine (155-181 hp), offering precise handling and a rear-wheel-drive layout. Available with a manual transmission, it’s loved for its driving dynamics.

    • Design:

  • Available in convertible and coupe forms, with a minimalist interior that prioritizes the driving experience.

    • Fuel Efficiency:

  • Offers excellent mpg (28-35), making it practical as well as fun.

    • Price:

  • Starting from around $26,000, it’s an affordable option for sports car enthusiasts.

    • The MX-5 is beloved for offering a pure, thrilling driving experience at a reasonable price, making it one of the best-selling sports cars ever.
